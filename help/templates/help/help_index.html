Help - Mama's Mart

Welcome to the Help section of Mama's Mart. Here you can find useful information and support to assist you in using the application effectively.

Getting Started

To get started, navigate through the dashboard and use the menu to access different sections such as Products, Inventory, Sales, and more.

Common Questions

  • How to add a new product? - Go to the Products section and click on "Add Product". Fill in the required details and save.
  • How to manage inventory? - Use the Inventory section to view stock levels, suppliers, and transfer products between warehouses.
  • How to view sales reports? - Access the Sales section to add, edit, or remove sales records and view reports.

Contact Support

If you need further assistance, please contact the support team at support@mamasmart.com.
